CATEGORY 1: GETTING STARTED & ACCOUNT
================================================================================

Q: How do I sign up?
A: Tap Sign Up → enter your email → check your inbox for a 6-digit code → done in under 60 seconds. You can sign up with any Nigerian email (Gmail, Yahoo, school email, etc.).

Q: I didn't receive my OTP code
A: Check your spam/junk folder first. If still nothing after 2 minutes, tap Resend Code. Make sure your email is typed correctly. If MTN/Glo/Airtel network is slow, the SMS may take a minute.

Q: My email isn't being accepted
A: Make sure you're using a real email (no typos like "gmail.con" or "yahooo.com"). School emails (.edu.ng) work fine.

Q: Can I sign up with phone only, no email?
A: Right now we need an email so we can send you order receipts and delivery codes. You can use any free email — Gmail takes 30 seconds to set up if you don't have one.

Q: I forgot my password
A: Tap Forgot Password on the login screen → enter your email → we'll send you a reset link. Check spam if you don't see it within 2 minutes.

Q: Can I change my email?
A: Yes — go to Profile → Settings → Change Email and confirm with the OTP we send.

Q: Why do you need my campus / school?
A: So we show you sellers from your own campus first — closer means faster delivery and easier returns. You can change campus anytime in Settings.

Q: My campus isn't on the list
A: We're adding new schools every week! Sign up anyway and pick the closest school — we'll notify you the moment your campus goes live.

Q: Can non-students use VendoorX?
A: Absolutely! Anyone in Nigeria can buy. We just started on campuses because that's where we have the strongest seller network.

Q: Is VendoorX free?
A: 100% free to sign up, browse, and list products. We only charge a small ₦100 platform fee per order, paid by the buyer at checkout.

Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Note: this permanently removes your wallet balance, listings, and order history. Withdraw any funds first.

CATEGORY 2: BUYING & ORDERS
================================================================================

Q: How do I place an order?
A: Find what you want → tap Buy Now → choose delivery or pickup → pay with card, transfer, or USSD → done! Your money sits safely in escrow until you confirm delivery.

Q: What payment methods can I use?
A: We accept all Nigerian payment options through Paystack:
- Debit/credit cards (Verve, Visa, Mastercard)
- Bank transfer
- USSD (*737#, *966#, *894# etc.)
- Bank account direct debit

Q: Can I pay on delivery?
A: We don't offer cash-on-delivery directly — but our escrow system gives you the same protection. You pay upfront, but the seller doesn't get the money until YOU confirm you've received and you're happy.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Most campus orders arrive same day (1–6 hours). Cross-campus or cross-state delivery takes 1–3 days depending on distance.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Go to Dashboard → My Orders → tap any order. You'll see the status (Pending → Shipped → Delivered) plus the seller's WhatsApp to chat directly.

Q: My order says "Shipped" but I haven't received anything
A: Reach out to the seller directly via the WhatsApp button on the order page. If they don't respond within 24 hours, tap Report Issue and our team will step in.

Q: I received the wrong item
A: Tap your order → Report Issue → choose "Wrong Item Received" → upload a photo. Our team reviews within 3 business days, and your money stays in escrow throughout.

Q: The item is damaged / fake / not as described
A: Same process: order → Report Issue → choose the reason → add photos. Don't confirm delivery until the issue is sorted. Your money is safe.

Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Yes, before the seller marks it as shipped — tap Cancel Order and you'll get an instant full refund. After it's shipped, you'll need to coordinate with the seller or report an issue.

Q: How do I get a refund?
A: If the order qualifies (item not delivered, wrong item, damaged), refund is automatic to your original payment method within 3–7 business days after dispute resolution.

Q: How long do refunds take?
A: Once approved: 3–7 business days for cards, 1–3 days for bank transfers. Paystack handles the actual transfer.

CATEGORY 3: ESCROW & DELIVERY CONFIRMATION
================================================================================

Q: What is escrow? / How does escrow work?
A: Escrow is our safety system. When you pay, your money goes into our secure wallet — NOT to the seller. Only after you confirm delivery (with a 6-digit code we send you) does the seller get paid. If something goes wrong, your money is fully refundable.

Q: How do I confirm delivery?
A: When the seller hands you the item, they'll ask for your 6-digit delivery code — we send it to your email + SMS the moment they mark "Shipped". Give them the code only after you've inspected the item and you're happy.

Q: I didn't get my delivery code
A: Check email + spam folder. Also check your in-app notification bell (top-right). If still missing, tap Resend Code on the order page.

Q: The seller is asking for the code before delivering
A: WARNING: NEVER share your delivery code before you've physically received and checked the item. Sharing the code releases their payment immediately. If they pressure you, decline and report it.

Q: What if I never confirm delivery?
A: After 7 days with no dispute, the system auto-releases the money to the seller (assuming they marked it as shipped). If the item never arrived, open a dispute BEFORE that 7-day window closes.

Q: Can I dispute after I've confirmed delivery?
A: Once you give the delivery code, escrow releases. Disputes after that point are harder but possible — contact support immediately with evidence.

CATEGORY 4: SELLING ON VENDOORX
================================================================================

Q: How do I become a seller?
A: From your dashboard → tap Become a Seller → fill in your name, campus, bank details → upload your first product → you're live in under 5 minutes! No fees, no KYC delays.

Q: Do I need a business name to sell?
A: Nope! You can sell as an individual student. If you want to use a brand name (like "Tomi's Sneakers"), just enter it in the seller-name field.

Q: How much does it cost to sell?
A: Listing is completely free. We only charge ₦100 per successful order, paid by the buyer (not you). You keep 100% of your asking price.

Q: How do I list a product?
A: Dashboard → Add Listing → upload 1–6 photos → write a title + description → set price → choose category → publish. Takes 2 minutes.

Q: How many products can I list?
A: Unlimited! Free accounts can have as many active listings as they want.

Q: How do I get more sales?
A: Top tips:
1. Clear photos — natural lighting, plain background, multiple angles
2. Detailed descriptions — size, color, condition, what's included
3. Fair pricing — check similar items on VendoorX first
4. Reply fast — buyers go to whoever responds within minutes
5. Boost listings — for ₦200–₦500, your item appears at the top of search

Q: When do I get paid?
A: The moment the buyer confirms delivery (or after 7 days with no dispute), the money lands in your VendoorX wallet. From there, withdraw to any Nigerian bank.

Q: How do I withdraw my earnings?
A: Dashboard → Wallet → Withdraw → enter amount → pick your bank → confirm. Money arrives in your bank within 5–30 minutes (Paystack instant transfer).

Q: Why is my withdrawal pending?
A: Withdrawals take 5–30 minutes normally. If yours has been pending for over 1 hour during banking hours (8am–6pm Mon–Fri), reach out and we'll check.

Q: Minimum withdrawal amount?
A: ₦1,000. Below that, your earnings stay in your wallet until you reach the minimum.

Q: What's the difference between "Available" and "Pending" balance?
A: Pending = orders not yet delivered/confirmed. Available = money you can withdraw right now.

Q: Can I sell services (haircuts, tutoring, designs)?
A: Yes! List them under the Services category. Set your price, schedule, and what's included.

Q: What can I NOT sell?
A: Forbidden items: drugs, weapons, alcohol (underage), counterfeit goods, stolen items, exam papers/leaks, ATM cards, account login info, sex services. Listing these gets your account banned permanently.

CATEGORY 6: DISPUTES & TRUST
================================================================================

Q: How do I report a seller?
A: Open the order → Report Issue → choose your reason (didn't deliver, wrong item, damaged, scam) → add details and photos. Our dispute team reviews within 3 business days.

Q: How long does dispute resolution take?
A: Up to 3 business days for a decision. Your money stays safely in escrow throughout — neither side gets it until we decide.

Q: What happens if I win the dispute?
A: Full refund to your original payment method within 3–7 business days. The seller's account may also receive a strike or ban depending on severity.

Q: How do you verify sellers?
A: We verify identity via BVN-linked bank accounts (only the bank account, not the BVN itself), email confirmation, and progressive trust scores based on completed orders + reviews.

Q: How do I avoid getting scammed?
A: Always pay through VendoorX (escrow protects you). Never send money directly to a seller's bank account. Never share your delivery code before receiving the item. Check seller ratings + reviews before buying. Report anything suspicious immediately.

Q: Someone is trying to scam me on WhatsApp
A: Don't engage. Screenshot the conversation, tap Report User on their profile, and forward the screenshot to our support. We ban scammers within 24 hours.

================================================================================

Q: I think someone hacked my account
A: Immediately: change your password → log out of all devices (Settings → Security → Sign Out Everywhere) → contact us. We'll freeze your wallet within minutes pending review.

Q: I'm getting login attempts I didn't make
A: Change your password right away and enable 2-factor authentication (Settings → Security → 2FA). We also email you every time a new device logs in.

Q: Can I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?
A: Yes! Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication. We send a code to your email or phone whenever you log in from a new device.

CATEGORY 8: TECHNICAL / APP ISSUES
================================================================================

Q: The app is slow / not loading
A: Try: 1) close and reopen the app 2) check your internet (try opening google.com) 3) clear cache (Settings → Clear Cache) 4) update the app to the latest version. If still broken, tell me what page is slow.

Q: I can't upload photos
A: Make sure each photo is under 5MB. Use JPG or PNG (not HEIC). If using iPhone, go to Settings → Camera → Formats → Most Compatible to default to JPG.

Q: Notifications aren't working
A: Go to your phone's Settings → Apps → VendoorX → Notifications and enable all. Also check inside the VendoorX app: Settings → Notifications.

Q: The chat / WhatsApp button doesn't open
A: Make sure WhatsApp is installed on your phone. If you're on web, you'll be redirected to WhatsApp Web — make sure you're logged in there.

Q: My listing photos are sideways
A: Re-upload using a landscape photo or rotate before uploading. We're improving auto-rotate in the next update.
